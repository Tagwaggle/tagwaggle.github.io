>_ About Joseph Guynes, MBA

Who I Am

My name is Joseph Guynes, and I am an experienced higher education instructor who focuses on students one by one. I love the feeling of helping a student and seeing that "ah-ha" moment. I hold an MBA, and a BS in Business Management, and I am currently working on a BS in Software Engineering. I am also currently working as an instructor in Power Skills for WGU.

One-liner mission: Helping students one by one.

My unique teaching style:

I teach students where they are—recognizing that not everyone comes to me with the same skills, so I have to adapt to them.

Tools & technologies I use:

I integrate SEL (Social-Emotional Learning) into all my instruction to create a holistic learning environment.

What I Believe

Learning Philosophy: I believe that everyone has the capacity to learn, given the right circumstances.

Why Creativity & Student-Centered Approaches Matter: You should always use all of your brain, not just the right or left—incorporate everything, and you will see success.
