Who I Am

Hi! I'm Joey Guynes, a Power Skills Instructor with a creative twist, a lifelong fan of 80s cartoons, and a a mission to help students find their confidence and shine

Why I Do It

Growing up, I was told, I wouldn't be smart enough to accomplish much. But with grit, curiosity, and a passion for learning, I've gone well beyond those expectations. Now, I'm here to help others do the same... to empower every student to become the best version of themselves, no matter what.

What I Bring

I bring creativity and outside-the-box thinking to everything I do, blending pop culture and a dash of humor to make learning engaging and fun. People say I'm always in a good mood, family-focused, and student-obsessed. Qualities I believe help me connect and support learners in meaningful ways.

What to Expect

When you learn with me, expect an environment where everyone is valued, where no question is too small, and where practical skills come with a side of retro fun. My goal is to help you build confidence, sharpen your skills, and enjoy the journey along the way.
